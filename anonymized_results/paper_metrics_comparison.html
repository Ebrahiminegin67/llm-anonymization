Verdict

  • Both architectures reach similar adversarial accuracy (55.0% baseline vs 55.0% parallel best).
  • Evidence rate after anonymization drops further under the parallel anonymizer: Attack B 30.0% vs baseline 50.0%. This means the parallel anonymizer removes more textual signals that the attacker relies on.
  • Utility is comparable: baseline 0.942 vs parallel 0.941.
